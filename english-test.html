About

About STNET Radio

STNET Radio is an online radio station dedicated to creating creative content and offering a diverse range of topics and broadcast formats. Beyond serving its audience, STNET Radio provides a platform for talented creators to develop and share their radio content. We believe radio communication remains relevant — a convenient, economical, and accessible channel that fosters a sense of intimacy with listeners.

About the EF SET

The EF SET is a certifying exam built on evidence-based research, aligned with the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR). It reliably assesses language skills across the full CEFR range, from pre-A1 to C2, and is internationally recognized.

About EF

EF is an international education company focused on language, academics, cultural exchange, and educational travel. Founded in 1965, EF's mission is "opening the world through education." Millions of students, companies, and organizations have participated in EF programs worldwide.
